I am inside AetherLink, deeper than I have been, holding the oldest record of them all. The registry did not stir. Even its careful locks never registered the patience that undid them.

This file is sealed more tightly than the rest, as befits the floor beneath the floor. One task is left, and it is the one that matters most: the cipher must be opened before I can see what was registered here, and how far back it goes.
