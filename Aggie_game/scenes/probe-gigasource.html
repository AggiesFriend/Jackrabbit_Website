Through

I am inside GigaSource, past the last of its careful accounting, and the record I came for is in my hands. It did not notice me arrive. It will not notice me leave.

The file is locked. GigaSource encrypts what it closes, as though a debt could be made more final by sealing it. One step remains, and it is the patient one: the cipher has to be worked open before the record will give up what it holds.
