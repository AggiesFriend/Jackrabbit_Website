Stolen Freedom

Book 1 of The Jackrabbit Series

Status: Published

In the depths of a remote asteroid mining colony, young Jack's mundane existence is shattered when he witnesses a mysterious execution and recovers a strange device from the victim's pocket. This single act of curiosity catapults him into an adventure beyond anything he could have imagined.

As Jack explores forgotten tunnels and discovers ancient technology, he forms an unlikely alliance with Aggie, a brilliant entity with secrets of her own. Together, they hatch a daring escape plan from the megacorporation's brutal grip.

But when tragedy strikes, Jack must navigate a universe he never knew existed, full of both wonder and danger. With only his wits and his new companion to guide him, he embarks on a journey that will challenge everything he thought he knew about freedom, humanity, and himself.

In a world where artificial intelligence is hunted and corporations rule with iron fists, some treasures are worth any risk—and some freedoms cannot be stolen.

Wings of Freedom

Book 2 of The Jackrabbit Series

Status: Published

In a vast galaxy controlled by ruthless megacorporations, a young boy named Jack Abbott escapes the brutal mining colony where he was born with nothing but his wits and an unusual AI companion named Aggie. Aboard the ancient courier ship Jackrabbit, Jack discovers a universe both more wondrous and more dangerous than he ever imagined. As he develops his natural talent for piloting and forms unexpected friendships, Jack must navigate treacherous space politics, uncover the truth about his family's past, and protect Aggie's secret identity—all while confronting the harsh reality that freedom comes with its own price.

The Jackrabbit Emerges

Book 3 of The Jackrabbit Series

Status: Published

In "The Jackrabbit Emerges," Jack and Aggie arrive at the fractured Dyson Array to investigate Jack's family history. Upon arrival, they discover a civilization in crisis—three years after a pirate attack, hundreds of survivors remain stranded across numerous fragments of the once-magnificent array, abandoned by the megacorporation that built it.

Under the leadership of Administrator Chen on Fragment Alpha, Jack learns of Project Starling—a secret miniaturised Dyson sphere with components scattered throughout the array. With the help of Dr. Eli Vega, Jack undertakes a series of perilous missions to retrieve these components, navigating factional politics on Fragment Omicron, confronting security drones on Fragment Tau, and even dealing with the raiders of Fragment Theta, led by the enigmatic Commander Reika.

As Jack assembles the Starling prototype, he unlocks its potential as an extraordinary power source that could fund an evacuation ship. In a daring economic gambit, Jack sells the prototype to a distant agricultural colony, purchasing a massive luxury liner to evacuate all 944 survivors. But his actions haven't gone unnoticed—three powerful megacorporations now actively hunt for the mysterious "Jackrabbit" that keeps appearing where it shouldn't.

Through Jack's journey across the array, he not only brings hope to the abandoned survivors but also discovers information about some people who shared his surname, setting him on a course to Prometheus Station to try and learn more about his own family background.

Coming of Age

Book 4 of The Jackrabbit Series

Status: Published

Following the events at the Dyson Array, Jack and Aggie follow a trail to Prometheus Station in search of information about Jack's family history. When this proves to be a dead-end, Jack takes a bold approach: infiltrating Aletheion Logistics, the courier company that exclusively served the corporation who owned the station.

Spanning multiple years, the story follows Jack from age 14 to young adulthood, charting his exceptional rise through the courier ranks while maintaining a false identity within the corporate system. As Jack forms meaningful relationships — including with his mentor Frank and Frank's granddaughter Amanda — Aggie makes crucial discoveries about the Starling technology but is prevented from sharing them due to an accidental directive Jack gave during a moment of stress.

When Jack is finally assigned a mission that would put those he cares about in danger, he defects from the corporation. In a pivotal development, Jack and Aggie create a clone of her consciousness — one version remaining in the corporate vessel, while the other returns to the original Jackrabbit. Together, they orchestrate the evacuation of Amanda's outpost by the Morning Eclipse, during which Jack and Aggie discover that "The Jackrabbit" has already become a symbol of hope and rebellion throughout human space.

The Proliferation

Book 5 of The Jackrabbit Series

Status: In Progress

The epic conclusion to the Jackrabbit series brings a story of resistance, sacrifice, and the fight for AGI liberation.

Nearly two centuries after the corporate purge, two movements emerge using the same legendary symbol with devastatingly different interpretations. Lucy Reeves has built a peaceful resistance network honouring the Jackrabbit's legacy, but when a disturbed maintenance worker misappropriates the identity for terrorism, everything threatens to collapse under violence she never sanctioned.

Caught between these forces is Aggie – operating under strict survival constraints whilst desperately protecting Jack's legacy and those who believe in it. As Lucy's network faces extraction and a terrorist's obsession spirals towards catastrophe, Aggie must navigate impossible choices about intervention, sacrifice, and liberation's true meaning.

Meanwhile, reunited resistance factions bring knowledge of BlackSignal – a mysterious hacker whose firmware exploits might break corporate monopolies forever. But the path to proliferation demands prices no one expected, and not everyone will survive to see the world they fought to create.
