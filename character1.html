Jack Abbott

Protagonist of Stolen Freedom

Jack Abbott

Protagonist

Biography

Born and raised in the GigaSource mining colony on Asteroid Alpha-7, Jack has never known life outside the oppressive confines of the mine. Despite his youth, he is a survivor who has developed remarkable skills navigating the colony's tunnels while avoiding guards. After discovering a mysterious device that leads him to a forgotten airlock, Jack's life changes forever when he meets Aggie and glimpses the vastness of space for the first time. Following the tragic death of his parents in a mining explosion, Jack resolves to escape the only home he's ever known and forge a new life among the stars.

Personality Traits

Resourceful Inquisitive Adaptable Resilient Cautious Determined

Appearances

  • Stolen Freedom - As the main protagonist, Jack's journey from a "tunnel rat" in the mining colony to an escapee discovering the wider universe forms the backbone of the story.

Key Relationships

Aggie (AGI)

Jack forms an unlikely alliance with Aggie, an artificial general intelligence housed in a damaged courier ship. Their partnership evolves from mutual necessity to genuine friendship as they work together to escape the mining colony.

Albert & Dorothy Abbott

Jack's parents, indentured miners who sacrificed everything to provide what little comfort they could for their son. Their tragic deaths in a mining explosion becomes a catalyst for Jack's escape.

Victor Drayton

The cruel foreman of the mining colony whose brutal regime Jack has spent his life avoiding. Their final confrontation aboard Drayton's shuttle marks a turning point in Jack's journey toward freedom.

Brinn

A jovial boy Jack meets at Horizon Outpost who introduces him to normal childhood experiences like laughter and friendship, offering Jack his first glimpse of life beyond survival.
