Key Relationships

Elena Vega

Initially distrustful of Elena's scientific approach, Alex gradually develops a deep respect for her brilliance and unwavering moral compass. Their complementary skills form the foundation of the resistance movement.

Commander Darius

Alex's former mentor turned nemesis. Their relationship is complicated by a shared history and mutual respect for each other's abilities, despite being on opposite sides of the conflict.

Maya Lin

Alex's most trusted friend and confidante. Their friendship dates back to their days at the Fleet Academy, and Maya's navigational skills perfectly complement Alex's piloting abilities.
