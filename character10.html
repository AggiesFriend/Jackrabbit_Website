Foreman Oswald ("Ozzy")

Supporting Character / Shipyard Foreman

Biography

Ozzy is the experienced, good-natured foreman of the shipyard at Horizon Outpost. With his weathered hands and encyclopedic knowledge of spacecraft, he quickly develops a fondness for both Jack and the ancient courier ship that comes to be named "Jackrabbit." His enthusiasm for working on rare, vintage spacecraft is matched only by his genuine concern for Jack's wellbeing. Beyond his official role repairing and upgrading the Jackrabbit, Ozzy serves as a valuable ally to Jack, connecting him with other useful contacts like Burke and helping him navigate some of the more complex aspects of life at Horizon Outpost.

Personality Traits

Knowledgeable Kind-hearted Enthusiastic Resourceful Supportive Discreet

Appearances

  • Wings of Freedom - Ozzy oversees the extensive repairs and upgrades to Jack and Aggie's courier ship, becoming a trusted ally who helps Jack navigate his new life at Horizon Outpost while introducing him to the underground community that operates in the outpost's "grey areas."

Key Relationships

Jack Abbott

Ozzy takes a paternal interest in Jack, offering guidance and support that extends well beyond his official duties as a shipyard foreman. He recognizes Jack's difficult past and appreciates his resilience, becoming one of the boy's most reliable allies on Horizon Outpost.

The Jackrabbit

Ozzy develops an almost reverential appreciation for the ancient courier ship, delighting in the opportunity to work on such a rare vessel. It's Ozzy who christens the ship "Jackrabbit," bestowing a name that reflects both its owner and its elusive capabilities.

Burke

Ozzy maintains a professional relationship with the reclusive engineer Burke, serving as an intermediary between Burke's shadowy operations and Jack's needs. Their connection hints at Ozzy's deeper involvement in Horizon Outpost's unofficial networks.
