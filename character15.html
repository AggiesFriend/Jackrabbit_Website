Administrator Mei Chen

Supporting Character / Fragment Leader

Biography

Administrator Mei Chen is the stern but capable leader of Fragment Alpha in the shattered remains of the Dyson Array. Serving as Senior Administrator for the entire array for fifteen years before the pirate attack, Chen's leadership and organizational skills have kept Fragment Alpha functioning at near-optimal efficiency despite being cut off from corporate support for three years.

Chen is known for her no-nonsense approach to problem-solving and her unwavering commitment to the survival of those under her authority. Her focus on structure and order has enabled Alpha to maintain higher standards than other fragments, including functioning hydroponics, manufacturing capabilities, and a disciplined security force. When Jack arrives with news of the Starling project and the possibility of evacuation, Chen sees the strategic value in this opportunity and commits Alpha's resources to the mission, despite initial scepticism.

Prior to the attack, Chen had a personal relationship with Dr. Eli Vega, which ended when he was transferred to another project. The revelation that Vega had been working on Project Starling without informing her created significant tension between them when they were reunited after the attack, though they gradually begin to reconcile as they work together on the evacuation plan.

Key Relationships

Dr. Eli Vega

Former romantic partner who kept his involvement in Project Starling secret from her due to corporate restrictions. Their past relationship creates tension during their reunion, but their shared goal of evacuating the cluster's survivors provides common ground for reconciliation.

General Singh

Chen's trusted military advisor and second-in-command. They share a long working relationship and often communicate through silent exchanges, demonstrating their deep professional understanding and trust.

Jack Abbott

Initially sceptical of the young pilot, Chen comes to respect Jack's unique abilities and contributions to the evacuation plan. She shows unexpected concern for his safety, recognising his importance to their mission's success.

Commander Reeves

Alpha's disciplined security chief who carries out Chen's orders with efficiency and loyalty. They maintain a professional relationship built on mutual respect and shared commitment to Alpha's survival.
