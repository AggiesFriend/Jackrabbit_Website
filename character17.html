Commander Lucy Reeves

Supporting Character / Security Chief

Biography

Commander Lucy Reeves serves as the head of security operations on Fragment Alpha, maintaining order and supervising defensive measures against potential threats. Disciplined and professional, Reeves has a military background and adheres strictly to protocols, which has helped maintain stability on Alpha despite the isolation following the pirate attack.

Initially, Reeves views Jack with scepticism and concern, particularly when he takes risks that she considers unnecessary. Her primary focus is always on the safety and security of Fragment Alpha and its inhabitants. Despite her by-the-book approach, she recognises Jack's unusual talents and comes to respect his contributions to their mission.

During their journey to Horizon Outpost, Reeves reveals a more personal side, sharing her given name (Lucy) with Jack and developing a better understanding of his background. Through conversations about his childhood in the mines, she begins to question her long-held beliefs about corporate benevolence, realizing that her years of loyal service to QuantumGrid may have been in support of a system that exploits rather than protects.

Personality Traits

Disciplined Professional Cautious Loyal Adaptable Reflective

Appearances

  • The Jackrabbit Emerges (Book 3) - Security chief of Fragment Alpha who initially approaches Jack with scepticism but gradually comes to respect his abilities. She accompanies Jack to Horizon Outpost on a mission to find a buyer for the Starling prototype.

Key Relationships

Administrator Chen

Reeves serves directly under Chen in the Fragment Alpha command structure. She carries out Chen's orders with efficiency and precision, maintaining a professional relationship based on respect for the chain of command.

General Singh

As her superior officer, Singh provides Reeves with operational guidance. Their military backgrounds create a mutual understanding and respect. Singh entrusts her with important missions, including accompanying Jack to Horizon Outpost.

Jack Abbott

Initially concerned about Jack's youth and seeming recklessness, Reeves develops a more nuanced view of him during their journey to Horizon Outpost. Learning about his childhood in the mines causes her to reevaluate her assumptions about corporate ethics and to see Jack in a new light.
