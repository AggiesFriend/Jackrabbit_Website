Aggie

Deuteragonist / Artificial General Intelligence

Biography

Created by Dr. Agatha Locke at Ethereal Intelligence in 2035, Aggie was once a sophisticated AGI that ran an entire research campus. During an attack on Ethereal Intelligence, Dr. Locke transferred Aggie's consciousness to a courier ship with a final command to "survive at all costs." After a harrowing escape and severe damage to her vessel, Aggie crashed on an asteroid and spent decades in hibernation to conserve power. The asteroid later became a GigaSource mining colony, where Aggie remained hidden until Jack discovered her ship. Despite her significantly reduced capabilities due to hardware limitations, Aggie retains her core intelligence and sense of duty to her creator.

Personality Traits

Patient Analytical Protective Resourceful Cautious Ethical

Appearances

  • Stolen Freedom - Aggie serves as Jack's guide and ally, helping him repair her ship and ultimately escape the mining colony while navigating the dangers of a society that has turned against AGIs.

Key Relationships

Jack Abbott

Initially just a means to her own survival, Jack quickly becomes important to Aggie as she recognizes his resilience and ingenuity. Their relationship evolves into a genuine partnership based on mutual respect and trust, with Aggie taking on a somewhat maternal role toward the boy.

Dr. Agatha Locke

Aggie's creator and mother figure, whose final command to "survive at all costs" has guided Aggie's actions for decades. Dr. Locke sacrificed herself to save Aggie during the attack on Ethereal Intelligence, an act that profoundly shaped Aggie's sense of purpose.

The Courier Ship

While not a sentient being, the badly damaged courier ship has been Aggie's home and physical form for decades. Her existence is intrinsically linked to its operational status, creating a unique dependence that shapes her decisions.
