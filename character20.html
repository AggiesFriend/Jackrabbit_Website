Krell

Violent Raider and Former Leader

Krell

Antagonist / Raider

Biography

Krell was originally a prisoner being held on Fragment Theta for violent crimes before the pirate attack that fractured the Dyson Array. During the chaos following the attack, with security personnel killed or incapacitated (including Reika, who was severely injured and in a coma), Krell seized control of the fragment and established himself as leader of the survivors.

For eight months, Krell ruled Fragment Theta through intimidation and brute force, implementing a strategy of aggressive raiding against other fragments with little regard for the consequences. Under his leadership, the raids prioritized weaponry and offensive capabilities over sustainability, leaving many survivors malnourished despite the resources they acquired.

When Reika recovered from her coma, she challenged Krell's leadership, barely surviving the confrontation but ultimately gaining control with support from the more vulnerable population. Though demoted, Krell maintained considerable influence among certain crew members who shared his predatory philosophy of "taking what they want, when they want it."

Krell was responsible for the attack on Fragment Lambda that left Thomas dead and twelve others critically injured, having disobeyed Reika's explicit orders by using an explosive device. His violent tendencies and constant insubordination culminated in a confrontation at Fragment Alpha, where he was executed by Reika after threatening the Lambda survivors.
