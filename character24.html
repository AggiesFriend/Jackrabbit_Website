Director Vera Santos

OmniFab Intelligence Division / Antagonist

Biography

Director Vera Santos heads OmniFab's Intelligence Division, reporting directly to Mr. Grey. She is responsible for coordinating the corporation's search for the Jackrabbit and analysing all information related to the vessel and its technology. Santos was the first to recognise the statistical anomaly when mentions of "Jackrabbit" began appearing across sectors.

Her methodical approach and analytical mind make her invaluable to OmniFab's operation, processing complex data with remarkable efficiency. Santos oversees the corporation's covert operatives and runs the technical analysis of evidence gathered from locations like Halcyon Fields, where she confirmed the presence of pre-purge technology.

While utterly loyal to OmniFab, Santos focuses on facts rather than corporate politics. This data-driven approach sometimes puts her at odds with the more politically-minded executives, but her exceptional analytical capabilities make her too valuable to ignore. Her meticulous reports on the Jackrabbit sightings help shape OmniFab's strategy for locating and neutralizing what they perceive as a significant threat to corporate stability.

Personality Traits

Analytical Methodical Efficient Fact-Focused Loyal Detail-Oriented

Appearances

  • The Jackrabbit Emerges (Book 3) - Introduced as the head of OmniFab's Intelligence Division, tasked with tracking down the Jackrabbit after identifying the pattern of its appearances across various sectors.

Key Relationships

Mr. Grey

Santos reports directly to Grey, providing him with the analytical foundation for OmniFab's strategic decisions regarding the Jackrabbit. Their professional relationship is based on Santos's ability to deliver clear, actionable intelligence and Grey's trust in her analytical capabilities.

OmniFab Operatives

Santos oversees a network of field operatives who gather information about the Jackrabbit's appearances. She maintains a professional, results-oriented relationship with these agents, evaluating their performance based solely on the quality and reliability of the intelligence they provide.

Corporate Intelligence Network

Santos has developed sophisticated algorithms and data analysis systems that allow her to sift through vast amounts of information to identify patterns related to the Jackrabbit. Her relationship with these systems is almost symbiotic, as she continuously refines and adapts them to improve their detection capabilities.
