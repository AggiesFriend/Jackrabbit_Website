Decker

Raider Second-in-Command / Supporting Character

Biography

Decker serves as Commander Reika's trusted second-in-command among the raiders of Fragment Theta. A tall, heavily muscled man with engineering expertise, he knows every inch of their vessel and every person on the fragment. His combination of technical knowledge and leadership abilities makes him an invaluable asset to the raider community.

When Reika's neurological condition worsens during the evacuation, Decker steps up to coordinate the raiders' role in transporting survivors to the Morning Eclipse. Despite his intimidating appearance, he demonstrates surprising diplomacy and adaptability, working effectively with Lucy Reeves and the other fragment leaders to ensure a smooth evacuation process.

Decker's most significant contribution comes when he discovers data storage modules hidden in their vessel's maintenance areaâ€”modules containing information about the Abbotts that he promptly delivers to Jack. After Reika steps down for medical treatment, Decker formally takes command of the newly christened "Reika's Bravery," honouring his former leader while helping complete the evacuation and guiding the raiders toward a more constructive role within the survivor community.

Appearances

Key Relationships

Commander Reika

Decker's relationship with Reika is built on mutual respect and trust. As her second-in-command, he supports her reform efforts within the raider community and steps up when her health deteriorates, continuing her vision while honoring her leadership by naming their vessel after her.

Jack Abbott

Initially cautious toward Jack as an outsider, Decker develops respect for him after witnessing his efforts to help the Dyson Array survivors. This respect leads him to share the discovered data modules about the Abbotts with Jack, providing crucial information for Jack's personal quest.

Lucy Reeves

Decker forms a working relationship with Lucy during the evacuation process, demonstrating unexpected diplomatic skills. Their cooperation helps bridge the gap between the raiders and other survivor groups, facilitating a more unified community aboard the Morning Eclipse.

Raider Community

As Reika's second-in-command and later as the leader of "Reika's Bravery," Decker guides the raiders through their transition from predatory practices to becoming contributing members of the survivor community, helping them find a new purpose and identity.
