Frank Kowalski

Veteran Aletheion Courier / Supporting Character

Biography

Frank Kowalski is a seasoned courier with thirty-four years of experience at Aletheion Logistics. His extensive career has given him unparalleled knowledge of corporate politics, shipping routes, and the intricate workings of the courier industry. Frank represents the wisdom and stability that comes from decades of dedicated service.

When Jack Abbott joins Aletheion as a courier, Frank takes him under his wing as both mentor and father figure. Having observed countless young couriers come and go, Frank recognises something special in Jack's abilities and dedication. He guides Jack through the complexities of corporate life, teaching him not just the technical aspects of courier work but also how to navigate the political landscape of a major corporation.

Frank's mentorship extends beyond professional guidance. Having raised a family whilst maintaining his courier career, he provides Jack with insights into balancing work and personal life. His relationship with Jack represents the paternal guidance that Jack never received, helping him understand what it means to have someone genuinely invested in his success and wellbeing.

Personality Traits

Experienced Mentoring Wise Protective Patient Loyal

Appearances

  • Coming of Age (Book 4) - Introduced as Jack's mentor and father figure at Aletheion Logistics, guiding him through corporate politics and the courier profession with his decades of experience.

Key Relationships

Jack Abbott

Frank serves as Jack's mentor and surrogate father figure, providing the paternal guidance Jack never had. Their relationship develops from professional mentorship into genuine familial affection, with Frank taking pride in Jack's rapid advancement through the courier ranks.

Amanda Kowalski

Amanda is Frank's beloved granddaughter, and he takes great pride in her achievements as a CyberNexus researcher. Frank is instrumental in bringing Jack and Amanda together, recognising that they would be well-suited for each other.

Eleanor Kowalski

Eleanor is Frank's wife and life partner, sharing his values of family loyalty and hard work. Together, they provide Jack with his first experience of a stable, loving family environment.
