Amanda Kowalski

CyberNexus Researcher / Supporting Character

Biography

Amanda Kowalski is a brilliant researcher at CyberNexus, specialising in neural networks and artificial intelligence systems. As Frank Kowalski's granddaughter, she has inherited both his work ethic and intellectual curiosity, channelling these traits into cutting-edge research in computational neuroscience.

Amanda's work at CyberNexus involves developing advanced neural network architectures and studying the intersection between biological and artificial intelligence. Her research is highly regarded within the corporation, and she represents the new generation of scientists pushing the boundaries of AI development.

When Jack Abbott enters her grandfather's life as a mentee, Amanda initially meets him with polite interest. However, as she gets to know Jack, she becomes genuinely attracted to his unique combination of natural talent, determination, and vulnerability. Their relationship develops into something deeper than friendship, with Amanda representing the possibility of a normal, stable life for Jack—something he has never experienced before.

Amanda embodies the path toward domesticity and conventional happiness that Jack has always secretly yearned for but never thought possible. Through her, Jack begins to understand what it means to have someone who cares for him not because of what he can do, but simply because of who he is.

Personality Traits

Brilliant Compassionate Independent Supportive Curious Grounded

Appearances

  • Coming of Age (Book 4) - Introduced as Frank's granddaughter and Jack's love interest, representing his opportunity for a normal life and genuine romantic connection whilst pursuing her own career in neural networks research.

Key Relationships

Jack Abbott

Amanda becomes Jack's love interest and represents his chance at a normal, stable relationship. Their connection grows from initial attraction to genuine love, with Amanda seeing past Jack's extraordinary abilities to appreciate the person beneath.

Frank Kowalski

Frank is Amanda's devoted grandfather who takes immense pride in her achievements. Their close relationship forms the foundation of the family dynamic that welcomes Jack, with Frank serving as the bridge between Amanda and Jack's relationship.

Eleanor Kowalski

Eleanor is Amanda's grandmother who has helped raise her with strong values and intellectual curiosity. Eleanor supports Amanda's relationship with Jack, recognising the positive influence they have on each other.
