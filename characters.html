Character Biographies

Meet the fascinating characters from The Jackrabbit Series, including those from both "Stolen Freedom" (Book 1) and "Wings of Freedom" (Book 2).

Jack Abbott

Protagonist

A resourceful young "tunnel rat" from a mining colony who discovers a way to escape his oppressive existence.

Full Biography

Aggie

Deuteragonist

An artificial general intelligence created by Dr. Agatha Locke who has been hiding in a damaged courier ship for decades.

Full Biography

Victor Drayton

Antagonist (Book 1)

The cruel and paranoid foreman of the mining colony who rules through fear and brutality.

Full Biography

Albert & Dorothy Abbott

Supporting Characters

Jack's parents, indentured workers whose love provides the only warmth in his harsh upbringing before their tragic deaths.
