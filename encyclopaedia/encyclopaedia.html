The Jackrabbit Encyclopaedia

A comprehensive guide to the technologies and concepts of the 23rd century

Explore the Future

Welcome to The Jackrabbit Encyclopaedia, your source for understanding some of the advanced technologies, systems, and concepts that shape human civilisation in the 23rd century. From faster-than-light communication and travel to artificial intelligence, these entries provide detailed insights into the world of The Jackrabbit series.

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)

Forbidden Consciousness Technology

Truly conscious, self-aware artificial entities capable of independent thought, creativity, and moral reasoning. Strictly prohibited throughout human space and subject to active suppression by corporate military forces.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Limited Autonomous Systems Technology

Automated systems that perform autonomous decision-making within strictly defined parameters. These sophisticated state machines execute predefined protocols without consciousness or self-awareness.

SnapMesh

Interstellar Communication Network

The galaxy's primary interstellar communication network, enabling faster-than-light data transmission across vast distances through a distributed mesh of autonomous relay nodes.

SnapSpace Travel

FTL Transportation Technology

Humanity's primary method of faster-than-light transportation, enabling near-instantaneous movement between distant points by transitioning through higher dimensional space.

Suits

Hazardous Environment Suits

A range of safety equipment which enables people to work in otherwise inaccessible environments.
